About Rescue Rover

Rescue Rover designs and manufactures solar-powered RC vehicles equipped with cameras and sensors for navigating tight and hazardous spaces. Our solutions are tailored for emergency response, medical applications, and industrial inspections, ensuring safety and efficiency in critical situations.

Features

  • Compact design to navigate tight spaces.
  • High-definition cameras for real-time video streaming.
  • Multi-terrain traction for various surfaces.
  • Environmental sensors for hazardous environments.
  • Solar-powered charging for extended usage.

Our Products & Services

Products:

  • RC Vehicles: Solar-powered units with cameras and multi-terrain capabilities.
  • Sensor Models: Units equipped with temperature, gas, and radiation sensors.

Services:

  • Control Platform: App for video streaming and sensor data analytics.
  • Leasing & Rentals: Flexible plans for temporary or periodic use.
  • Maintenance & Support: Regular servicing and 24/7 tech support.
